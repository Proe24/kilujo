Additive Wreck Content

Astra's Wrecks

A lightweight pack that adds wrecked versions of several stock-style ships that vanilla never had wrecks for — including Thalamus-infested variants for a nastier salvage.

Wrecks Includes Thalamus versions By AstraAegis
What it adds
7 wrecks: Kessel, Azimuth Type 2 Okayu, Orca, Remora, Venture, Destiny, Kessel-B — including Thalamus versions of some.
Footprint
Very lightweight (~186 KB); rounds out vanilla ships that lacked wrecks.

⚑What to expect

  • Thalamus variants turn some of these wrecks into living-ship encounters — expect organ-like hazards and defenders, not just empty salvage.
  • Some early versions had minor issues (Orca husk pathing, airlock gaps); the author has addressed several.
  • Stacks freely with Shipwrecks Extended and the beacon packs.
