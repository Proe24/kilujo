Additive Beacon Content

Even More Beacon Stations

A small, punchy pack of 8 beacon stations — 5 originals plus 3 bandit variants. Larger stations are built to be easy to fix... but one of them hides a nasty surprise.

Submarine / Mission Contains a nuclear depth-charge trap By Nade Spammer
What it adds
8 beacon stations: 5 original + 3 bandit-themed variants. Larger ones are designed to be easy to repair.
Compatibility
Additive; confirmed compatible with Beacons Extended and the other beacon packs.
Nuclear depth-charge docking trap. One station has a docking trap with a nuclear depth charge that has wiped entire permadeath/ironman crews almost instantly. There are meant to be light indicators/alarms — dock cautiously and watch for warning signs before committing your whole crew.

⚑How to use it

Enable it; the stations enter the beacon rotation. One commenter reported severe lag when firing a roof station's mining pod in early-game Cold Caverns — worth noting on lower-end machines. Otherwise treat them like any beacon-activation mission.
