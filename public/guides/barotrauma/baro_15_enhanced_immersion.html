Immersion & Quality of Life

Enhanced Immersion

Polishes the feel of the sub: interaction sounds, a calmer ambient mix, ID-scanner door textures, wireable decor, a zoomed-in camera, and "zDoors" that open hidden background rooms. The audio/visual layer works for everyone; the camera and zDoors need client-side Lua.

Requires LuaCsForBarotrauma Item / Environment Zoom: 1080p or lower only
What it is
An immersion/QoL mod by _]|M|[_ (Lua code largely by Evil Factory; camera by zomgtehderpy; config menu by jia).
Dependency
Client-side LuaCs for the [Lua] zoom camera + zDoors. Every non-Lua feature works without it.
Load position
Below other mods (Any mod → Enhanced Immersion → New Wrecks). Below Immersive Diving Gear if you run both.
Lighter option
"Enhanced Immersion (lua only)" (id 3123289876) gives just the camera/zDoors/cursor, omitting the sound/texture/stat overrides.

📷The zoom camera

  • Default: the camera is zoomed in much further than vanilla, for a more connected feel.
  • Aiming a weapon zooms back out so you're not restricted in combat (some players dislike this auto-zoom-out).
  • Periscope view gets only a tiny default increase — buy periscope upgrades to improve it.
  • Adjust via the in-game config menu. Exact per-option names aren't published.
Known issue: the zoom only works correctly at 1920×1080 or lower — at 1440p/4K it misbehaves. This is the cause of nearly all "zoom doesn't work" reports.

🚪zDoors

zDoors let you "enter doors in the background." In reality they teleport you to a separate enclosed room placed elsewhere on the sub (an illusion of a layered room), or act as shortcuts between existing rooms. NPCs/bots can't use them.

  • To build your own: place a destination room, wire two zDoors together, and hide the wires for the illusion.
  • EI includes 2 zDoor modules that only spawn with DynamicEuropa.
  • zDoors were broken for a long time (teleported you "into the door like a seat"); a fix shipped Jan 2025 — update the mod if they misbehave.

⚠Gotchas

  • Zoom is resolution-locked to ≤1080p.
  • Combat diving-suit / exosuit changes make suits feel slow; a reported bug stops Mantis Genes' walk-speed buff applying while wearing an exosuit. The "lua only" version avoids all stat/sound/texture overrides.
  • Sounds/interactions are lost on items overridden by other mods.
  • The door ID-scanner texture isn't individually toggleable — use the "lua only" version to skip it.
  • Load below Immersive Diving Gear to keep both.
