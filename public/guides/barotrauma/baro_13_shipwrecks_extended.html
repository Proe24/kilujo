Additive Wreck Content

Shipwrecks Extended

Adds a large batch of new shipwrecks to the level pool — some dangerous, some harmless, some you can even dock with. Pure salvage variety, compatible with every wreck mod.

Mission / Environment Compatible with all wreck mods Sibling to Beacons Extended
What it adds
Many new wrecks (some built with Jlobblet's Sub-Wrecker tool). Variety of danger levels; some are dockable.
Compatibility
Compatible with all currently known wreck mods, explicitly including "More Wrecks for Barotrauma." Per DynamicEuropa's dev, wreck mods are "generally always compatible" with DE.

⚑How to use it

Enable it and the new wrecks appear during salvage and exploration. Remember that with Barotraumatic installed, wrecks are now populated with monsters — send a prepared dive team, not a lone scavenger. Pairs with Beacons Extended and Astra's Wrecks.
