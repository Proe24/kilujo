Shrink a photo

Drop one or more photos below. Each one is resized, compressed, and stripped of location data — all in your browser. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. When it's done, click Save and drag the file into Pages CMS.

iPhone HEIC files?

This tool reads JPEG, PNG, and WebP. iPhone HEIC photos aren't supported here — easiest fix is to use Squoosh.app instead (it handles HEIC and runs entirely in your browser too). Or change your iPhone Camera setting to Most Compatible so it shoots JPEG by default: Settings → Camera → Formats → Most Compatible.

Stays entirely on your device. No uploads, no analytics.
