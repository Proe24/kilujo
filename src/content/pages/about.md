Mid-West meets East Asia. Quiet Kentucky farmlands, steeped in generations of family history, intertwine with the vibrant pulse of San Francisco, echoing tales of Taiwan and Southern China.

Our journeys, both geographic and personal, paint a canvas as diverse as our family. Kilo, our spirited but selectively affectionate Shiba Inu, joined us during our travels through Nebraska, while Kujo, our Jindo/Border Collie mix, found his way into our hearts all the way from Korea.

This is our story, captured through the lens of our everyday adventures, the connections we make between cultures, and the simple beauty we discover along the way.

— Kilo, Kujo, Gianna & Stephen
